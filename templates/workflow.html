Step 01

Search and narrow

The client starts on the public side, opens the fleet, and compares only vehicles that match the trip.

Next step

Client actions

  • Open the Cars page and review all available vehicles.
  • Use category, fuel, and status filters to reduce the list.
  • Check the vehicle details page before starting the booking.

System response

  • Shows updated vehicle data directly from the database.
  • Keeps car details, price, and description visible in one place.
  • Prepares the selected vehicle for the reservation request page.
Step 02

Confirm reservation

After choosing a car, the client enters trip dates and sends a reservation request for admin approval.

Next step

Client actions

  • Pick start and end dates on the reservation page.
  • Review estimated total and send the request.
  • Wait for admin validation and payment recording.

Admin actions

  • Receives a real-time notification in the admin panel.
  • Checks the request, then accepts or updates the reservation.
  • Follows payment, guarantee, and finance items when needed.
Step 03

Track completion

Once the car is on the road, the team keeps the reservation open until the vehicle is returned and the admin closes it.

Back to homepage preview

Operations follow-up

  • Track reservation status from pending to accepted to ended.
  • Keep maintenance, guarantees, and fixed-charge alerts visible to the admin.
  • Protect vehicle availability so the fleet stays accurate for the next client.

Client visibility

  • Clients can open reservation history to see active and past bookings.
  • The reservation remains active until the admin marks it as ended.
  • History stays organized for future follow-up and reporting.
